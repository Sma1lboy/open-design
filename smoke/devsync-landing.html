Common questions
Does DevSync store my repo contents?+

No. DevSync reads metadata only — branch names, PR state, CI status — via your existing OAuth scopes. Code never leaves your Git host.

How does it handle timezones across a team?+

Every scheduling decision respects the attendee list's intersection of working hours. DST boundaries are normalized on both ends of the recurrence.

What if I don't want my calendar rewritten automatically?+

Turn on "propose-only" mode. DevSync will suggest moves as silent drafts in your inbox — you approve, nothing shifts without your nod.

Can I self-host?+

Yes. The core scheduler is AGPL and runs on a single Postgres + one worker. Enterprise tier adds SAML, regional data residency, and 24/7 support.

Which calendar providers are supported?+

Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, iCloud, and Fastmail today. Proton and self-hosted CalDAV arrive this summer.
